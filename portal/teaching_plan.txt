### Week 1: Introduction to Algebra

*   **Curriculum Outcome:** Understanding variables and expressions
*   **School Curriculum:**
    *   Define variables and constants.
    *   Translate word phrases into algebraic expressions.
    *   Evaluate algebraic expressions by substitution.
*   **Activities:**
    *   Interactive session: What is Algebra?
    *   Practice: Translating phrases to algebraic expressions.
    *   Group activity: Evaluating expressions with given values.
*   **Book Recommendation:**
    *   Title: Algebra 1 for Dummies
    *   Description: Chapter 1 - Variables and Expressions
*   **Online Resources:**
    *   Khan Academy: Introduction to Algebra
    *   [https://www.khanacademy.org/math/algebra/introduction-to-algebra](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/algebra/introduction-to-algebra)

### Week 2: Simplifying Algebraic Expressions

*   **Curriculum Outcome:** Simplifying expressions by combining like terms
*   **School Curriculum:**
    *   Identify like terms in algebraic expressions.
    *   Combine like terms to simplify expressions.
    *   Apply the distributive property to simplify expressions.
*   **Activities:**
    *   Demonstration: Combining like terms with visual aids.
    *   Practice: Simplifying expressions using the distributive property.
    *   Group activity: Simplifying complex algebraic expressions.
*   **Book Recommendation:**
    *   Title: Algebra 1 for Dummies
    *   Description: Chapter 3 - Simplifying Expressions
*   **Online Resources:**
    *   IXL: Simplify algebraic expressions
    *   [https://www.ixl.com/math/grade-7/simplify-algebraic-expressions](https://www.ixl.com/math/grade-7/simplify-algebraic-expressions)

### Week 3: Solving One-Step Equations

*   **Curriculum Outcome:** Solving one-step equations using inverse operations
*   **School Curriculum:**
    *   Understand the concept of equality.
    *   Solve one-step equations using addition, subtraction, multiplication, or division.
    *   Check solutions to equations.
*   **Activities:**
    *   Interactive session: The concept of equality and inverse operations.
    *   Practice: Solving one-step equations.
    *   Group activity: Creating and solving one-step equations.
*   **Book Recommendation:**
    *   Title: Algebra 1 for Dummies
    *   Description: Chapter 4 - Solving Equations
*   **Online Resources:**
    *   Mathway: Equation Solver
    *   [https://www.mathway.com/Algebra](https://www.mathway.com/Algebra)
